# Terms of Service

## 1. What the Service is

PoolMaster offers two kinds of functionality:

**(a) Analytics and information.** We aggregate publicly available data about liquidity pools across supported decentralised exchanges and blockchains, group it by trading pair, estimate returns using our own simulation and backtesting methods, and let you track wallet positions across multiple addresses.

**(b) A non-custodial transaction interface.** The Service lets you build and submit transactions — including opening, adding to, withdrawing from, closing, and otherwise managing liquidity positions, and claiming fees — against third-party protocols, using your own wallet.

**You initiate and authorise every transaction.** The interface prepares transaction data; **you** sign and broadcast it from your own wallet. Transactions execute on a public blockchain and are irreversible. Some transactions route through a smart contract we deploy (see Section 6); the underlying liquidity protocols are always third-party contracts that we do not own or control.
