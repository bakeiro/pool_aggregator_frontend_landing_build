How SushiSwap pools work

Liquidity pools on SushiSwap allow providers to deposit token pairs and earn fees on every swap. Concentrated liquidity lets LPs define price ranges to maximise capital efficiency.

The fees shown represent earnings generated in the last 24 hours. Pools with higher volume relative to their TVL tend to offer better returns, though they may carry higher impermanent loss risk.

Ethereum is one of the leading networks in the DeFi ecosystem, with SushiSwap among the top DEXs by volume. The pools listed here represent the most liquid and active pairs at the time of the last build.
